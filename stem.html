The Framework

What is STEM?

STEM represents Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Rather than learning them as separate subjects, we teach them as a unified language for problem-solving.

  • S Science: Discovering why things happen.
  • T Technology: Using tools to solve tasks.
  • E Engineering: Designing the solution.
  • M Mathematics: Calculating for precision.
